About Miami Munchies


A Service for Students

Miami Munchies was founded by Miami Juniors in the Spring of 2018. They were finding it very difficult to prepare healthy, quick meals for themselves while also managing schoolwork, and social lives. They decided to create an affordable food delivery program which delivers bi weekly recipes, and locally sourced ingredients for Miami students to prepare in their own kitchens. Today, Miami Munchies is used by over 1,000 Miami Students.
